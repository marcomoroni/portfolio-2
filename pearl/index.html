A tiny puzzle game for the Global Game Jam 2017

The theme for the Global Game Jam 2017 was waves and because it was my first Game Jam I decided to keep it simple. I participated with a friend and we had a lot of fun!

Here's how you play it: by pressing the directional arrows you move all the movable pieces on the grid by 1 step in the direction you chose. Your goal is to place all the pearls back to the corresponding shell.
